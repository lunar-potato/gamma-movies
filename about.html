About Us

Gamma Movies

"Gamma movies is a dynamic movie search web app that allow users to navigate easily and filter their personal choices. It is a user friendly app which can be easily navigated by most age groups including the elderly. If you prefer a light or dark theme screen, our navbar offers just that with a theme-switcher.
Another key-feature is a watchlist button that allow users to save their choices to their local storage and be able to see what movies they have saved. Our advanced movie filter feature allows users to filter movies according to their prefered genre, age rating, year release date, cast and crew information, ratings and more.."
